About Talk Tuk Tuk

Talk Tuk Tuk helps tourists, expats and Cambodians connect through language. Learn basic Khmer phrases quickly — or practise your English — with short video clips of real words and phrases used every day in Cambodia.

No boring lessons. No textbooks. Just pick a category, watch a short clip, and start talking. Whether you're haggling at the market, ordering food, or just want to make your tuk tuk driver smile — Talk Tuk Tuk has you covered.
